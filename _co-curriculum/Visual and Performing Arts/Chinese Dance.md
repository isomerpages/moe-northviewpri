<h4><strong>Chinese Dance</strong></h4>
<p>Chinese Dance CCA aims to develop in our members an appreciation of the
arts with a strong emphasises on character development. We seek to nurture
our dancers into confident and graceful individuals who are passionate
about dancing. Our members learn folk and modern Chinese Dances. Our members
hone their dance skills and techniques through perseverance and determination.
Our members will present their best during every performance.</p>
