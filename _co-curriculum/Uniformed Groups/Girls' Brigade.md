<p>The Girls’ Brigade is a value-driven uniformed group that aims to develop
every girl to her fullest potential by equipping, empowering and enabling
every girl to be a leader. Through the Girls’ Brigade Programmes, the girls
will be given the opportunity to lead and serve by example – putting others
before self.</p>
